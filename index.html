    Dunedin History Museum

    Graphic Designer and Exhibit Designer

    I created "Pieces of the Past: The History of Dunedin in 40 Objects" to highlight some of my favorite artifacts I got to work with at DHM. I photographed each object, removed the backgrounds in Photoshop, and used Photoshop to lay out each page. After writing all of the copy to go along with the objects, I created the front and back covers in Photoshop, and assembled the book as a PDF. I used Blurb to print it. The book is still for sale in DHM's gift shop.
